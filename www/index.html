Our Features

The Moxtra SDK is a comprehensive communication SDK that enables messaging, voice, and screen sharing straight from within your app.
It's available for iOS, Android, and Web and can be integrated with just few lines of code. Your users can instantly host a Moxtra Meet from your app,
and their friends can join from your app, the Moxtra app, or any browser (without an account!).


Screen Sharing

Our screen sharing enables your users to show your app to anyone - even those who don't have your app yet! With a single click, users can start a Moxtra Meet from within your app and invite their friends to show and tell. Invites can be sent by SMS, email, or an in-app notification. Desktop screen sharing includes support for multiple monitors.

Flexible Integration

Integrate within a wide range of applications, including mobile apps that do not use any backend infrastructure. Our SDK has readily pluggable UI elements requiring minimal integation effort for both mobile and web applications. Participants can join from anywhere, without any sign-up overhead, requiring no development effort.

Voice Call & Mobile Chat

With our voice calling, users can initiate a 1 on 1 or group voice call. Invitees can join from any device using your app, Moxtra app, or from any browser, without logging in. The host can opt to control the audio of the participants to manage the discussion. Moxtra Chat is a rich messaging capability for 1 on 1, group or topic based conversations.
