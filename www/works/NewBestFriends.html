New Best Friends

Mobile App
App Details

Status: Live

Type: iOS

New Best Friends is a recently released storybook app aimed at new readers. Through animated and narrated stories, it introduces kids to technology while teaching reading skills and positive life lessons. Moxtra was integrated into New Best Friends at an early stage, in order to give the app owner a better tool to show off her new app.

This is one of the primary strengths of the Moxtra SDK. Rather than tell someone about the app, or try to explain it in an abstract way, Moxtra gives app owners the ability to show the app directly to others. The result is similar to watching from over someone’s shoulder, except that you can do it from the other side of the world! Whether demoing the app, making a presentation, or just trying to teach your friend, the Moxtra SDK is the best communication tool available for mobile apps. In this case, introducing the app and explaining how to use the deep playback settings and child-safety options was vital for engaging tech-wary parents.

While Moxtra won’t be a primary feature for New Best Friends users (after all, their target demographic is only around 6 years old), the connectivity provided by the Moxtra SDK remains a powerful asset. Imagine enabling a child to read-along to their parent who is away on business! As with so many things, it’s those little moments that make it worthwhile, and we are excited about any opportunities for Moxtra to overcome distance to make these connections.
