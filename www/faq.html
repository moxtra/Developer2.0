FAQ

What is Moxtra?

Moxtra is a power communications platform for real-time engagement. With just a few lines of code, the Moxtra SDK enables you to integrate rich communication tools in your app within minutes. Whether it’s adding messaging, voice calling, screen-sharing, or interactive documents, Moxtra is the best way to make your app powerful and get ahead of the game.


How will Moxtra benefit my app?

Your app is already amazing. Why add communications? Real-time engagement will enhance your app, help you drive downloads, and maximize retention. Build a community within your product and attract new audiences organically. Gain a significant competitive advantage with minimal technical and financial involvement.


What communication capabilities can I build into my app with Moxtra?

We currently offer the following communication capabilities:

Chat | Our messaging is packed with features such as content sharing and group messaging capabilities. It is the perfect utility for your messaging needs.

Screen Sharing | Moxtra’s screen sharing enables your users to show off your mobile or web app to anyone on any device - even to people who don't have your app yet!

Real-Time Audio Conferencing | Empower your users with a rich voice call experience. Calls can be made between two individual people or as a group. Users can join from any device using your app, Moxtra’s app, or from any browser.

Stay tuned for several more features being added in our upcoming SDK releases.


What are some examples of Moxtra integrations?

Moxtra is the best way to add power communications to your app. The following is a list of just a few potential use cases for our SDK.

Content Sharing Apps | Enable your users to show off their creations made with your application. Whether its a simple sketch or complex equations, users can invite anyone to view their work and talk about it in real-time.

Collaborative Decision Making | Equip your app with instant content collaboration. Whether it’s an invoice or a sales forecast, you can discuss your content with the right people and make decisions while on the go. your app yet!

Student Collaboration Apps | Applications built for students can leverage Moxtra’s communication tools to build a truly collaborative environment. Give students the ability to discuss in real time and share annotated notes over content discussed in class

Learning Management Systems | Learning Management Systems serving schools and enterprises can add collaboration capabilities to classes by providing a rich real-time based discussion capabilities, enriching the overall learning experience.

Service Oriented Apps (e.g. Tax, Financial, Real-Estate) | Take customer engagement to the next level by using Moxtra’s highly interactive mediums of communication within your app. Enable your service professionals to interact with your customers with just a click of a button.

Gaming Apps | Use Moxtra’s communication stack to make gameplay more interactive. Allow your users to communicate with each other within your game to build a social gaming experience.

How does Moxtra work?

The Moxtra SDK provides a pluggable UI element that securely connects and delivers Moxtra services from the cloud. Install our SDK and have chat, screen sharing, audio conferencing, file sharing and annotation services built into your app within minutes. Let us deal with security, archives, search, authentication, and offline messaging so you don’t have to.



Will Moxtra affect my application’s backend infrastructure?

Not at all! Moxtra SDK integration is client side only. It won’t impact your app’s backend.
